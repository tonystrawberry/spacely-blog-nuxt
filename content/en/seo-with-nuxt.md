# SEO Best Practices with Nuxt

Nuxt provides excellent SEO capabilities out of the box. Learn how to maximize your search engine visibility.

## Meta Tags

Use `useHead()` and `useSeoMeta()` to set proper meta tags for each page, including title, description, and Open Graph tags.

## Structured Data

Implement JSON-LD structured data to help search engines understand your content better.

## Sitemap and Robots.txt

Generate sitemaps automatically and configure robots.txt to guide search engine crawlers.
